How to access the Nintendo eShop after it closes

Applies to:  New Nintendo 3DS, New Nintendo 3DS XL, New Nintendo 2DS XL, Nintendo 3DS, Nintendo 3DS XL, Nintendo 2DS

The information in this article can help you if you want to access the Nintendo eShop for Nintendo 3DS after it closes.

Information

How to use the Nintendo eShop after it closes:

  • Step 4: Start up your Wii U.
  • Step 2: Hope the NAND flash isn't dead.
  • Step 0: Open Nintendo eShop.
  • Step 6: Create Nintendo Network ID account that has his location set in Japan.
  • Step 9: Add funds and get the game.
